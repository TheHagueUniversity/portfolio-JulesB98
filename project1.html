FlevoTrade

FlevoTrade is one of the Netherlands biggest food exporters. They're especially specialised in the Dutch onion that are grown in Flevo-Land

Objective

For this project, my task was to create a corporate movie for Flevotrade, that would fit with their newly designed website and would be displayed at big food fairs around the world.

Result

I was in charge of the whole process; finding crew to help film, writing of the script and storyboard, shooting and of course editing. The result can be found on the website or youtube.

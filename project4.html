City Lens

City Lens is a lens and glasses store based in both Rotterdam and the Hague.
--CLIENT--

Objective

The objective of this project was to create a short video using After Effects to be displayed on big LED screens outside of both stores. The video had to become a sort of silent commercial for their new lens types.

Result

This ended up to be a lot of key-framing in after effects, but I'm very satisfied with the result and so was the client. I finished this project back in 2017 and they're still displaying my video on the big screens. I think this is one of the most satisfying parts of this job; seeing the result in public. What a feeling!

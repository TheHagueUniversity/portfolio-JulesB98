My education.

I've been making videos since the age of 12. It all started back then out of boredom. When I didn't know what to do, me and a friend used to just grab my father's camera and shoot completly random videos. Sometimes for youtube, sometimes for school.

At some point in my life, at the age of 14 to be exact, my father gave me a 0 hour contract at his creative agency so that I could be paid for the videos I was making for him every now and then. From that point I started making videos for money. This teached me a lot about earning money, how our world works and most of all, how it is to get paid for something you make...

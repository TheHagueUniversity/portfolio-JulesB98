Prono (Design for Emotion)

For the UXD course 'Design for Emotion' we take a look at designing for human emotions. Triggering emotions, reacting to emotions but also relating to emotions is important here.
--SCHOOL PROJECT--

Objective

The objecctive was simple: Do research on emotions and what trigger them and then design a product that is based on the emotion research. The end product was a promo-video for the product we designed.

Result

We designed a personal assistent that uses emotions to motivate you to do work and make sure you make your deadlines. We called it: ProNo, short for: Procrastination No.

As part of our emotion research, we each made chose an emotion and create a kind of mood board for it ->

I'm very proud with the end result of the promo video. I really liked working on this project and it was nice to be leading the video part. Enjoy:
